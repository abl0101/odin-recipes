How to Make Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 3 dashes bitters
  • 1 tsp water
  • 2 oz bourbon
  • Orange Peel

Steps

  1. Add sugar, bitters, then water into a rock glass
  2. Stir until sugar dissolves
  3. Add ice cube or sphere
  4. Add bourbon
  5. Stir
  6. Garnish with orange peel
